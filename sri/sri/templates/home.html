Welcome To The Software Research Institute

The SRI was established in 2006, with the goal to develop a leading edge research facility in AIT, and to act as a catalyst to spur innovation through applied research collaboration with industrial partners. The SRI conducts applied research in digital media communications and applications, and network and infrastructure management.

Since its foundation the SRI has been successful in attracting applied research and technology transfer funding valued in excess of â‚¬6 Million. The institute currently has 25 staff including post-docs, graduate students and experienced IT & SW Engineering staff.

The centre has built a reputation for applied research in the communications and network management domain and has collaborated extensively both with small and medium enterprises (SME) and multi-national companies. We are focused on bringing leading edge research to the market-place.

The SRI is also a partner of the Irish Centre for Cloud Computing and Commerce (IC4) which is an industry led national technology centre charged with helping Irish industry excel in cloud computing. The SRI conducts applied research in cloud security. Other partners are the University College Cork and Dublin City University.
